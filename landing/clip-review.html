How To Use · macOS

01
Download and install
Download ClipReview-mac.dmg . double-click it . drag ClipReview onto the Applications arrow . eject the DMG .
02
Open Clip Review
Open Applications . double-click ClipReview . first launch takes ~30 sec . browser opens automatically every time after .
If macOS shows "unverified developer" → System Settings → Privacy & Security → Open Anyway . one-time step .
03
Insert the ZEDREC card
Plug in the card reader . the volume appears in the drive selector automatically .
04
Review clips
Select the ZEDREC volume . all clips load with durations . click any clip to play it .
05
Submit handover
Once reviewed go to spec-ops.best to submit your shift handover .

How To Use · Windows

01
Download the .exe
Download SPEC-OPS-Clip-Review-win.exe . save it anywhere . Desktop works fine .
Edge warning "isn't commonly downloaded" → click ··· next to the file → Keep → Keep anyway . one-time only .
02
Run it
Double-click the .exe . if Windows SmartScreen appears → "More info" → "Run anyway" . browser opens automatically .
03
Insert the ZEDREC card
Plug in the card reader . the drive appears in the selector automatically .
04
Review clips
Select the ZEDREC drive . all clips load with durations . click any clip to play it .
05
Submit handover
Once reviewed go to spec-ops.best to submit your shift handover .
