how to use . macOS

01
Download and install
Download ClipReview-mac.zip . double-click to unzip . drag ClipReview.app to your Applications folder .
If macOS blocks the app . click Done on the warning . then open System Settings → Privacy & Security → scroll down → click Open Anyway . enter your password . the app will open normally from then on .
02
Double-click and go
Double-click ClipReview.app . browser opens automatically . a Desktop shortcut is created for next time .
03
Insert the ZEDREC card
Plug in the card reader with the ZEDREC SD card . the volume appears in the Drive Selector dropdown automatically .
04
Select drive and review
Pick the ZEDREC volume . all clips load . check active and idle hours in the summary bar . click any clip to play it .
05

what it reads

Drive detection
Scans connected drives for the ZEDREC volume . no manual path needed .
IMU activity
Reads linear acceleration from /top-camera/imu . high stddev = active shift . low stddev = static footage .
Active / idle hours
Summary bar shows fractional hours across all clips . use it to confirm a full active shift was recorded .
Video playback
Converts MCAP clips on the fly . left camera view . plays directly in the browser tab , no extra software .
24h heatmap
Timeline grid showing coverage, idle footage, short clips, and gaps across the full recording window .
No install
FFmpeg and the server are fully bundled . Windows: drag out the .exe and run . macOS: drag .app to Applications and double-click .
